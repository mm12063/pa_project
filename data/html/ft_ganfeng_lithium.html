About the Financial Times

The Financial Times, one of the world's leading business news organisations, is recognised internationally for its authority, integrity and accuracy.

FT content is produced each day by over 500 journalists reporting from the world's political and commercial nerve centers. It provides an authoritative and impartial picture of international trade, finance, the economy and politics.

Providing extensive news, comment and analysis, the newspaper is printed at 24 print sites across the globe, has a daily circulation of 421,026 (ABC figures, February, 2009) and a readership of 1.3 million people worldwide in print and online.

About the Financial Times Archive

The FT has created this archive to offer both long-term digital preservation and on-line accessibility for our subscribers. This on-line library allows subscribers to access our newspaper content, in its original format, from the convenience of their standard web browser. This repository hosts millions of articles and is open to full-text search with unprecedented effectiveness.

The FT archive begins with our 28 January 2009 issue and will be updated daily.
